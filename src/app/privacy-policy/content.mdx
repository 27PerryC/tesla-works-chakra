
# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: December 21, 2025*

Tesla Works is a student organization affiliated with the University of Minnesota. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how information is collected and used when you visit our website or participate in our programs and events.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Personal Information You Provide

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, including but not limited to:

- Name and email address when you subscribe to our newsletter
- Information submitted through contact forms or interest forms
- Information provided when registering for events or membership
- Responses to surveys or feedback forms

Providing this information is voluntary. You may choose not to provide certain information, though doing so may limit your ability to participate in some activities.

---

### Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

- IP address
- Browser type and device information
- Date and time of access
- Pages visited and referring URLs

This information is collected for basic site functionality, security, and performance monitoring.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- Communicate with members and interested students
- Send newsletters, announcements, and event updates
- Respond to inquiries and requests
- Organize events and manage club activities
- Improve our website and outreach efforts
- Comply with applicable university policies or legal obligations

We do not sell or rent personal information to third parties.

## 4. Disclosure of Your Information

We may share information in the following circumstances:

- **With Your Consent:** When you explicitly agree to information sharing
- **University Administration:** When required by university policies or oversight
- **Service Providers:** With trusted third-party services used to operate the website or manage communications
- **Legal Requirements:** If required to comply with applicable laws or lawful requests

We only share information to the extent necessary for these purposes.

---

## 5. Data Security

We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect the information we collect. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have the right to:

- Request access to personal information we hold about you
- Request correction or deletion of your information
- Withdraw consent for communications such as newsletters

Requests may be made using the contact information below.
